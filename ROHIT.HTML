Welcome to Pollution Awareness

Learn about different types of pollution and their impact on the environment.

Air Pollution

Causes

Industrial emissions, vehicle exhaust, etc.

Effects

Respiratory problems, global warming, etc.

Solutions

Reducing emissions, using public transport, etc.

Water Pollution

Causes

Industrial waste, agricultural runoff, etc.

Effects

Harm to aquatic life, health risks, etc.

Solutions

Treating wastewater, reducing plastic use, etc.

Soil Pollution

Causes

Use of pesticides, industrial waste, etc.

Effects

Reduced fertility, health hazards, etc.

Solutions

Organic farming, proper waste disposal, etc. Document
